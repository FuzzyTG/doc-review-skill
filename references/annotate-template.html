Q2 Product Strategy Review

Draft v0.3 · April 2026 · Confidential

Executive Summary

Our Q1 performance exceeded expectations with a 23% increase in monthly active users and a 15% improvement in retention rates. The AI-powered features launched in February contributed to a 35% increase in daily user engagement, with the smart suggestion engine seeing a 40% adoption rate within the first month.

However, we observed a concerning trend in our enterprise segment. Churn rate increased from 4.2% to 5.8%, primarily driven by competitors offering more robust API integrations. This needs immediate attention in Q2.

Key Initiatives for Q2

We propose three major initiatives: First, launching a comprehensive API platform that allows enterprise customers to build custom integrations. Second, expanding our AI capabilities with real-time collaboration features. Third, entering the Southeast Asian market with localized versions for Vietnam, Thailand, and Indonesia.

The API platform is our highest priority. Based on customer interviews, 67% of churned enterprise accounts cited "lack of integration flexibility" as their primary reason for leaving. We estimate that a well-designed API could recover 30-40% of these accounts.

Resource Allocation

We recommend shifting 20% of the mobile team to API development for the next two quarters. This will delay the planned iOS widget feature by approximately 6 weeks, but the revenue impact of reducing enterprise churn far outweighs the consumer feature delay.

Total budget request for Q2 initiatives: $2.4M, broken down as API Platform ($1.2M), AI Features ($800K), and APAC Expansion ($400K). This represents a 15% increase over Q1 spending but is projected to deliver 3x ROI by Q4.

Risk Assessment

The primary risk is execution speed. Our competitors are not standing still — Company X announced their API beta last week, and Company Y is reportedly hiring aggressively for their APAC expansion. We have a 3-month window to establish our position before the market becomes significantly more competitive.
